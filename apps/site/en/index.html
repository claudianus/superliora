Problem

Long tasks lose context and scatter decisions

Complex implementations, migrations, and refactors blur the goal, lose evidence, and repeat the same questions. More tools often mean more broken flow.

Keep plan, impact surface, and verification in one continuous thread.

Documentation & knowledge

Ultra workflow

Execution from plan to verification

Shift-Tab Ultrawork mode gathers source-backed research first, narrows complex requests through UltraPlan interview, creates a true/false-verifiable UltraGoal, then proceeds through Swarm decision, integration, and verification.

Research 01

Checks current sources before the interview creates question options.

UltraPlan 02

Interviews requirements, constraints, and risks until completion can be judged clearly.

Swarm decision 03

Records ENGAGE or DEFER before specialist subagents are invoked.

Install

Install from source and start with liora

The installer builds SuperLiora from source and sets up the independent liora command. Use curl on macOS/Linux or the PowerShell one-liner on Windows.

$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/claudianus/superliora/main/install.sh | bash
PS> irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/claudianus/superliora/main/install.ps1 | iex
$ liora
